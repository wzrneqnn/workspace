## What You're Actually Getting

Google Workspace isn't just Gmail with a logo swap. Under one subscription you get Gmail, Drive, Meet, Calendar, Chat, Docs, Sheets, Slides, Forms, Sites, Keep, NotebookLM, AppSheet, Gemini AI — the whole stack. Everything talks to each other, everything lives in the browser, and you don't need IT to set most of it up.

The headline feature for small businesses? **Custom email at your domain.** Instead of `yourbusiness.gmail.com`, people email `alex@yourbusiness.com`. It costs less than most people think, and it makes your business look real in a way that a free Gmail address just... doesn't.

At $7/user/month on annual billing, it's almost embarrassingly cheap for what you get. The 30 GB pooled storage is the main limitation; if your team is generating a lot of files, you'll outgrow it.
